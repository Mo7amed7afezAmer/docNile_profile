Medical Data Collection

We partner with hospitals, clinics, and healthcare institutions to ethically collect diverse clinical data at scale. Every data point is gathered in compliance with local and international regulations.

  • IRB/ethics board approved protocols
  • Informed consent management

  • Scalable collection infrastructure

Data Annotation & Labeling

Our team of clinical experts and trained annotators deliver accurate, consistent labels for medical imaging, clinical text, pathology slides, and structured data — ready for machine learning consumption.

  • Board-certified clinical reviewers
  • Multi-annotator consensus workflows
  • Radiology & pathology annotations
  • NLP-ready clinical text labeling
  • Custom taxonomy support
  • Quality assurance at every stage

Data Cleaning & Standardization

Raw clinical data is messy. We transform it into clean, structured, AI-ready datasets using proven pipelines for de-identification, normalization, and format standardization.

  • Automated de-identification (HIPAA/GDPR)
  • DICOM, HL7, FHIR format support
  • Duplicate detection & resolution
  • Missing data handling strategies
  • Cross-institutional normalization
  • Audit trails & data provenance
