About Us

Building the Foundation for
Trustworthy Medical AI

We believe AI in healthcare should be built on data that represents everyone — ethically collected, rigorously validated, and fully de-identified.

Mission

To provide ethically sourced, diverse, and de-identified medical data that enables the development of trustworthy, inclusive AI healthcare solutions.

Vision

A world where AI-powered healthcare is trained on data representing all of humanity — leading to equitable health outcomes for everyone, everywhere.

Values

  • Ethics First: Patient consent and privacy above all
  • Diversity: Representation for all populations
  • Privacy: De-identification at the core
  • Quality: Research-grade standards in every dataset

Our Story

DocNile was founded on a simple but urgent observation: the AI models shaping healthcare's future are trained predominantly on data from North America and Western Europe. This creates a dangerous blind spot — algorithms that work well for some populations but fail for billions of others.

We saw both a problem and an opportunity. Diverse populations across emerging markets represent hundreds of millions of people with unique genetic profiles, disease patterns, and healthcare contexts that are largely absent from global AI training datasets.

Working closely with healthcare institutions, we've built the infrastructure to ethically collect, de-identify, annotate, and deliver clinical datasets that meet the highest international standards while ensuring patient privacy and informed consent at every step.

Today, DocNile partners with leading AI companies, research institutions, and healthcare organizations to bridge the global medical data gap — one dataset at a time.
